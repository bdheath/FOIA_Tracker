// These are valid tags for use in eFOIA templates:

[@DATE]		- The current date (system generated)
[@REALNAME]	- The user's real name (stored in the user's preferences)
[@ADDRESS]	- The user's address (stored in user preferences)
[@PHONE]	- Ths user's phone number (stored in user preferences)
[@EMAIL]	- The user's e-mail address (stored in user preferences)
[@TO]		- The address block from the letter-generating form
[@GREETING]	- The greeting from the letter-generating form
[@RECORDS]	- The description of records sought from the letter-generating form
		  (for simplicity of formatting, eFOIA automatically removes any closing
		  period from the user's input.)
